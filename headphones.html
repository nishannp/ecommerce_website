Premium Wireless Headphones

Escape into your audio world with our Premium Wireless Headphones. Experience superior sound quality, powerful noise cancellation, and exceptional comfort, designed for all-day listening.

Immersive Sound & Active Noise Cancellation

Our custom-tuned drivers deliver rich, detailed audio with deep bass and crisp highs. Advanced Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) technology blocks out distractions, letting you focus on your music, podcasts, or calls.

  • Hybrid ANC with multiple microphones
  • Adjustable noise cancellation levels
  • Transparency mode to hear your surroundings
Exceptional Comfort & Design

The over-ear design with plush, breathable earcups and an adjustable headband provides a comfortable fit for extended wear. The headphones are lightweight and foldable, making them easy to take with you on the go.

Long-Lasting Battery & Seamless Connectivity

Enjoy up to 30 hours of playtime with ANC off, and up to 20 hours with ANC on. A quick 10-minute charge provides up to 5 hours of listening. Bluetooth 5.2 ensures a stable and reliable wireless connection.

Intuitive Controls & Crystal-Clear Calls

Easily control your music and calls with intuitive touch controls (or physical buttons, depending on the variant). The built-in microphone with noise reduction technology ensures clear voice calls, even in noisy environments.
