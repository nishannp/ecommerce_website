Advanced Fitness Tracker

Take control of your health and fitness journey with our Advanced Fitness Tracker. This sleek and stylish wearable provides comprehensive tracking of your activity, sleep, and heart rate, empowering you to make informed decisions about your well-being.

Comprehensive Activity & Health Tracking

Monitor your daily activity levels, including steps, distance, calories burned, and active minutes. Track your workouts with multiple sport modes and use connected GPS to map your routes. 24/7 heart rate monitoring and SpO2 (blood oxygen) tracking provide valuable insights into your cardiovascular health.

  • Automatic Exercise Recognition
  • Personalized Daily Goals
  • Active Zone Minutes
Advanced Sleep Analysis

Gain a deeper understanding of your sleep patterns with detailed sleep stage tracking (light, deep, REM) and a daily Sleep Score. Identify areas for improvement and develop healthier sleep habits.

  • Sleep Mode (Dims Display & Silences Notifications)
  • Smart Wake Alarm
Stay Connected & Motivated

Receive call, text, and app notifications directly on your wrist. Stay motivated with reminders to move and personalized insights. Choose from a variety of customizable clock faces to match your style.

Designed for Comfort and Durability

The lightweight and comfortable design is perfect for all-day wear. The bright color touchscreen is easy to read, even in direct sunlight. With a long battery life of up to 7 days, you can focus on your goals without worrying about frequent charging. Water resistant up to 50 meters, you can wear it in the shower or pool.
